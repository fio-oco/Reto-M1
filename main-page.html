Wanderlust

Have you been day dreaming about your next big trip? Wanderlust is a simple page to help you think about the places you'd love to travel to and help you build your travel bucket-list. Use our random generator to get some information on the capital, official languages etc. of different countries and some links to websites with useful information to get started on your planning!

This page was created by Asier Valtierra and Fiona O'Connell as part of the Code4Jobs Fullstack Web Development Bootcamp in Bilbao, October 2023.
